## Priority Issues

### [P1] Current degraded producer state is rendered as healthy

**Why it matters:** The Jul 12 scheduled marker says `degraded / market_source_prior_date` and the PIT still ends Jul 11, yet the sketches show an ambient freshness whisper and the prose says true overnight data arrives with the Jul 12 fire. This is exactly the last-known-as-fresh failure the document bans.

**Fix:** Record the failed Jul 12 fire, compose the current banner/dashed market lane, and distinguish latest successful market vintage from current refresh health.

**Suggested command:** `$impeccable harden`

### [P1] Full-Universe rank hierarchy has two focal values

**Why it matters:** The contract alternately calls overall rank and position rank focal, and the row presents both as DG values. A manager cannot know which number anchors the list.

**Fix:** Choose one focal value per scope and demote the other to explicitly labeled context with lower weight.

**Suggested command:** `$impeccable clarify`

### [P1] Mobile removes information needed to decode the lanes

**Why it matters:** DG is labeled, market is only an open circle, and mover copy drops `grew`/`shrank`. Users must remember lane identity and infer whether `+4pp` is a change or a current gap.

**Fix:** Label both lanes and preserve direction-bearing movement verbs on every viewport.

**Suggested command:** `$impeccable adapt`

### [P1] Mobile row templates contradict their own slot contract

**Why it matters:** Controls and gaps move between lines, captions describe the opposite arrangement, and `band +7pp` turns the quiet within-band state back into magnitude drama.

**Fix:** Pin one two-line template per row type and render either `within band` or a signed gap, never both.

**Suggested command:** `$impeccable layout`

### [P1] The morning answer is still an operations paragraph

**Why it matters:** Seven facts plus `pre-model` appear before the first mover. This exceeds a five-second scan and makes the surface feel like model monitoring rather than fantasy management.

**Fix:** Lead with gap movement and materiality; move market/model attribution and coverage taxonomy into a secondary line or receipt using manager prose.

**Suggested command:** `$impeccable distill`
