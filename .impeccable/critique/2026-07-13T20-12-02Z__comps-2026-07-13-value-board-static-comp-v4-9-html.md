## Priority Issues

### [P1] Movement content hashes do not enforce a valid evidence pair

**Why it matters:** A semantic Jul-09 mutation still exits 0 and changes the movement tape; a one-row synthetic capture can become the prior endpoint and also exits 0. The extractor prints only a 16-hex digest prefix, compares it to no expected manifest, and labels all 12,201 rows as `renderable payloads` even though only 334/340 are renderable on Jul-09/11.

**Fix:** Bind each frozen run to full expected digests and a complete-snapshot manifest, compare before emitting facts, and abort on mismatch or incomplete capture. Label the count as total payloads unless it is actually filtered.

**Suggested command:** `$impeccable harden`

### [P1] No-read receipts retain a nonexistent gap-rounding claim

**Why it matters:** Bam Knight and Braelon Allen correctly say there is nothing to compare, but the same card then says the gap rounds from a stored delta. That is an internal contradiction in the evidence layer.

**Fix:** Model the receipt as a discriminated row state and set every field atomically. A one-lane row should render rounding as not applicable, not inherit the generic two-lane rule.

**Suggested command:** `$impeccable harden`

### [P1] Mobile FA scope is invisible but keyboard-focusable

**Why it matters:** At 390x664 and 320x700 the radiogroup collapses to a 2px frame with zero client height while its selected 44px radio remains tabbable behind `overflow:hidden`. Keyboard users land on an invisible control and sighted users cannot change or confirm scope.

**Fix:** Make the toggle non-shrinking (`flex:none` / `min-height`) inside the scroll phone, preserve its focus outline, and retest after scrolling and at 200% text.

**Suggested command:** `$impeccable adapt`
