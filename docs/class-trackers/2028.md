# 2028 NFL Draft Class Tracker

## Purpose

Lightweight ground-truth reference for the 2028 NFL Draft class. Initialized in May 2026 because 2028 1sts are already active trade currency in dynasty markets — we cannot value them without a baseline file to anchor research against.

**Scope at this stage:** intentionally minimal. Heavy class-shape work begins after the 2026 college season produces meaningful junior-year data on 2028-eligible prospects.

## Status (May 2026)

- **Class shape:** unknown. Multiple analyst boards reference 2028 prospects but no consensus tier structure exists yet.
- **Eligibility universe:** players in their second-year-removed-from-HS cohort or earlier who have not declared. Roster turnover at the college level over the next 18 months will materially reshape this list.
- **Pick valuation guidance for the Trade Calculator:** treat 2028 1sts with **maximum uncertainty discount** until at least November 2026. Engine A should not assert any specific class-strength multiplier on 2028 picks at this time.
