# 2027 Class — Analyst Notes

## Logging Rules

- Every entry must include the analyst, the publication, and the date.
- Direct quotes must be ≤15 words and in quotation marks per copyright handling.
- Comparisons (player-to-player) are logged as **analyst comparisons**, never restated in our own voice.
- When an analyst note is cited inside a trade pitch or product surface, the citation must include "(analyst opinion — not engine output)."
